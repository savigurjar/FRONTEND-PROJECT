ABOUT THE CREW

The Straw Hat Pirates are more than just a crew—they’re a family bound by loyalty, trust, and shared dreams. No matter the challenges, they stand together, supporting each other with unwavering love, making everyone feel like they belong.

ASL - Ace Sabo Luffy

To Luffy, Sabo and Ace are his true brothers, connected not by blood but by an unshakable bond of love and loyalty. They shape his heart, give him strength, and are the family he cherishes most, always standing by him in spirit and guiding his journey forward.

STRAWHAT FAMILY

The Straw Hat Pirates are Luffy’s chosen family, each member representing something vital—Zoro’s loyalty, Nami’s guidance, Usopp’s courage, Sanji’s care, Chopper’s healing, Robin’s wisdom, Franky’s innovation, Brook’s joy, and Jinbe’s honor. Together, they give Luffy the strength and support to pursue his dream of becoming the Pirate King.

Gear 5th

Gear 5 is Luffy’s awakened form of his Devil Fruit, unlocking his full potential as the successor to Joy Boy. In this form, Luffy gains incredible power, cartoonish flexibility, and the ability to reshape his environment with imagination, embodying the spirit of freedom and laughter, much like Joy Boy.
